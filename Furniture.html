Furniture Castings

Furniture castings are key components in the creation of durable and stylish furniture. These castings are used to form the structural elements of furniture like chairs, tables, and cabinets. Manufactured from strong materials such as cast iron or aluminum, they offer robustness and elegance to furniture designs.

The process involves pouring molten metal into molds to create parts that provide both aesthetic appeal and functional strength. Furniture castings can be found in both modern and vintage designs, contributing to the stability and design integrity of the furniture.

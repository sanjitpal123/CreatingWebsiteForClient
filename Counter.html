Counter Weights

Counter weights are vital components used in mechanical systems, primarily to balance or offset a load, reducing the amount of force needed to operate machinery. These weights are commonly used in cranes, elevators, and other heavy-lifting equipment to provide stability and control during operations.

Made from durable materials like cast iron or steel, counter weights are carefully designed and manufactured to ensure precise performance. They help to improve efficiency and safety by maintaining proper balance and reducing stress on the system.
